About Me

Research-focused PhD scholar in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Jodhpur, specializing in medical image analysis, self-supervised learning, and world-model–based representation learning. Demonstrated experience in the design, implementation, and evaluation of deep learning frameworks for medical image segmentation and classification, with research published in reputed journals and scholarly book chapters.

Currently engaged in developing JEPA-style predictive learning frameworks and transformer-based world models to learn structure-aware and transferable representations with minimal dependence on labeled data. Possess strong technical expertise in deep learning, vision transformers, self-supervised learning, and medical imaging, with a well-defined trajectory toward building robust, data-efficient AI systems for healthcare.
